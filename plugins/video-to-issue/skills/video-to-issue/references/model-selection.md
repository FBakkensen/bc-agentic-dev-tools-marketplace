# Model Selection Guide

| Model | Speed | Accuracy | VRAM | Best For |
|-------|-------|----------|------|----------|
| tiny | Fastest | Basic | ~1GB | Quick tests, clear speech |
| base | Fast | Good | ~1GB | Default choice, balanced |
| small | Medium | Better | ~2GB | Important transcriptions |
| medium | Slow | High | ~5GB | Complex audio, accents |
| large-v3 | Slowest | Best | ~10GB | Critical accuracy needed |
